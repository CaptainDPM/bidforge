2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Service.
  • To process uploaded documents through AI models (Anthropic Claude) to generate proposal content.
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, billing receipts).
  • To enforce plan limits and prevent abuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your uploaded documents or generated content to train AI models.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not serve advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate BidForge:

  • Anthropic — AI model provider. Uploaded document text is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — Payment processing. Credit card data is stored and processed by Stripe, not by us. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery. Email addresses are shared with Resend solely to deliver emails you request.
  • Railway / cloud hosting — Infrastructure hosting. Data is stored on servers operated by Railway in the United States.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data and project data for as long as your account is active. You may delete individual projects at any time. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at dmarq@marquartsolutions.com.

Billing records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by law.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (TLS), hashed passwords (bcrypt), and access controls. However, no system is completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials.

Do not upload documents containing classified information, ITAR/EAR-controlled data, or sensitive PII unless you have confirmed appropriate security controls are in place.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access, correct, or delete your account information by contacting us.
  • Export your project data using the CSV export feature.
  • Request deletion of all your data by emailing us.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you may have additional rights under GDPR. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Cookies

BidForge uses browser localStorage to store your authentication token. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for business use and is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify account owners by email of material changes. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
